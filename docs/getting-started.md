# Getting Started with Clawdbot

Welcome to Clawdbot! This guide will help you get up and running in under 5 minutes.

## First Steps

### 1. Access Your Workspace

You received an email with your workspace credentials:
- **Workspace ID**: Your unique identifier
- **Access URL**: Direct link to your dashboard
- **API Key**: For programmatic access (optional)

Click the link in your welcome email or visit your access URL.

### 2. Connect Your Tools (Optional)

Clawdbot works great out of the box, but connecting your tools makes it even more powerful:

#### Gmail & Calendar
1. Go to **Settings** ‚Üí **Integrations**
2. Click **Connect Gmail**
3. Authorize with Google OAuth
4. Done! Clawdbot can now read emails and check your calendar

#### Slack
1. Settings ‚Üí Integrations ‚Üí **Connect Slack**
2. Choose your workspace
3. Authorize
4. Clawdbot can now post updates and respond to mentions

#### More Integrations
- Browser automation
- Web search (built-in)
- File access (coming soon)
- Custom integrations via API

### 3. Talk to Your Assistant

Simply start chatting! Try:
- "What's on my calendar today?"
- "Summarize my unread emails"
- "Remind me to call John tomorrow at 2pm"
- "Search the web for pricing on AWS Lambda"

## Usage Tips

### Best Practices
1. **Be specific**: "Schedule a call with Sarah next Tuesday at 3pm" is better than "schedule call"
2. **Use memory**: Tell Clawdbot about your projects, goals, and preferences
3. **Set up heartbeats**: Let it work in the background checking things for you
4. **Customize SOUL.md**: Make your assistant match your working style

### Common Commands
- **Memory**: "Remember that I prefer morning meetings"
- **Reminders**: "Remind me to X at Y time"
- **Email**: "Summarize emails from this week"
- **Calendar**: "What's my schedule tomorrow?"
- **Web**: "Search for news about AI"
